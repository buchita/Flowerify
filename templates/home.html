Flowerify

About


Flowerify is a web application that enables a user to input an image of a flower and verify its name. The system will upload, process, analyse the image, output the name and provide general care information for the identified flower with reference to database. The preprocessing and classification task will be undertaken using python Keras. The app will also assist in the maintenance and upkeep of the flower such as when and where to plant this flower; possible pests and diseases. The aim of the app ‘Flowerify’ is to help users to detect, recognise and understand flowers.
